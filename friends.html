Behind the Scene of our friendship
This is my best friend at Rembau Campus. Back during the COVID pandemic, I met them online. Because we are separated from our families, being friends with them has taught me a lot about life. Since day one, being treated properly and respectfully has brought us closer. I really adore our friendship. I will definitely miss them when we will done our Diploma soon. We plan to travel a lot when we want to distract ourselves from being stressed by doing a lot of assignment. It really helps us to release our tension just by talking about things that made us happy. Everything will look easy whenever we are together. I don't know what I would have done If I hadn't had my best friend beside me.
